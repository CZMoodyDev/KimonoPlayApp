OBI

Obi refers to the large sash that is tied around the kimono to hold it in place, while acting as a complimentary decorative ornament. The obijime is a long cord that is tied around the obi which holds the obi in place ensuring that the entire kimono stays tied together. The obi can be tied in a variety of styles.
